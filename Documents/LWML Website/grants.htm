Mite Goal

Mites - coins, dollars, and checks - collected in mite boxes fund mission grants.  At the LWML Washington-Alaska District Convention in June 2014, delegates selected mission grant recipients for the 2014-2016 biennium totaling $89,000.

With God, ALL things are possible!  With God, we WILL reach and exceed our mite goal!
